# SiteSentinel

> Detect website integrity issues before they impact your business. "Because trust should be monitored, not assumed."

SiteSentinel is an enterprise-grade website integrity monitoring platform designed to continuously assess website trust, security, SEO health, and advertising policy compliance.

Unlike traditional vulnerability scanners, SiteSentinel focuses on **website integrity** by combining automated scanning, rule-based detection, risk analysis, and AI-assisted interpretation into a single trust platform.

---

## Vision

Our vision is simple:

> Help businesses detect website integrity issues before search engines, advertising platforms, or customers do.

Website owners shouldn't discover problems only after:

- Google Ads account suspension
- Search ranking penalties
- SEO spam injections
- Malware infections
- Hidden keyword manipulation
- Policy violations

SiteSentinel continuously monitors websites and provides actionable intelligence before these issues become business problems.

# Product Philosophy

SiteSentinel is **not just a scanner.**

It is a **Website Trust Platform**.

The objective is not to generate reports.

The objective is to answer one simple question:

> **Can I trust my website today?**
