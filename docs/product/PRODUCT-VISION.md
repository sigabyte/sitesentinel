# Purpose

This document defines the vision, mission, objectives, scope, and guiding principles of the SiteSentinel platform.

It serves as the primary reference for understanding why SiteSentinel exists and what long-term value it aims to deliver.

---

# Vision

To become the world's most trusted platform for continuously monitoring website integrity, identifying hidden threats before they impact businesses, and providing clear, explainable, and actionable intelligence.

---

# Mission

SiteSentinel empowers businesses to continuously verify the integrity of their websites through deterministic analysis, intelligent risk assessment, and transparent reporting.

Our goal is to help organizations detect security, SEO, compliance, and policy-related issues before search engines, advertising platforms, or customers discover them.

---

# Core Question

Every feature developed within SiteSentinel should contribute to answering one fundamental question:

> **Can this website be trusted today?**

---

# Product Philosophy

SiteSentinel is not simply a website scanner.

It is a Website Trust Platform.

Rather than generating isolated scan results, SiteSentinel continuously evaluates multiple indicators to provide a clear understanding of a website's integrity and trustworthiness.

---

# Product Objectives

The primary objectives of SiteSentinel are:

- Continuously monitor website integrity.
- Detect hidden threats before they impact business operations.
- Provide explainable and evidence-based findings.
- Reduce business risk caused by website compromise.
- Help organizations maintain trust with customers and digital platforms.
- Enable proactive rather than reactive website management.

---

# Target Users

SiteSentinel is designed for organizations that depend on their websites as critical business assets.

Primary audiences include:

- Small and Medium Businesses
- Enterprise Organizations
- Digital Agencies
- Marketing Teams
- SEO Agencies
- IT Departments
- Managed Service Providers
- Website Administrators

---

# Problems We Solve

Many website owners only discover problems after significant business impact.

Examples include:

- Google Ads policy violations
- SEO spam injections
- Hidden malicious content
- Search engine penalties
- Website reputation damage
- Unauthorized website modifications
- Suspicious external integrations

SiteSentinel aims to identify these issues before they become business problems.

---

# Product Values

Every decision made within SiteSentinel should reinforce one or more of the following values:

- Trust
- Transparency
- Accuracy
- Explainability
- Reliability
- Simplicity
- Long-term Maintainability

---

# What SiteSentinel Is

SiteSentinel is:

- A Website Integrity Monitoring Platform
- A Website Trust Assessment Platform
- A Continuous Monitoring System
- A Decision Support Platform
- A Risk Assessment Platform

---

# What SiteSentinel Is Not

SiteSentinel is not:

- An antivirus solution
- A penetration testing platform
- A vulnerability scanner
- A Web Application Firewall (WAF)
- A SIEM platform
- A replacement for secure software development practices

---

# Long-Term Direction

The long-term vision of SiteSentinel is to become the trusted source of truth for website integrity.

As the platform evolves, it will expand its capabilities while remaining focused on one objective:

Helping organizations continuously understand, measure, and protect the trustworthiness of their digital presence.

---

# Success Definition

SiteSentinel succeeds when users no longer ask:

"Has something gone wrong with my website?"

Instead, they confidently know:

"The current integrity status of my website is verified, monitored, and continuously protected."

---

# Closing Statement

Trust should never be assumed.

Trust should be continuously measured, verified, explained, and protected.

SiteSentinel exists to make that possible.

> **Because trust should be monitored, not assumed.**
