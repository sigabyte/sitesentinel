# SiteSentinel Project Governance

## Roles

### Product Owner / Founder
Responsible for product direction, commercial positioning, priorities, and final decisions.

### Technical Product Architect
Responsible for architecture discipline, technical decision support, risk analysis, documentation structure, and implementation guidance.

## Working Discipline

- Architecture before implementation
- Decisions must be documented
- No major feature without scope definition
- ADR for important architectural decisions
- SSAS as the source of truth
- Engineering Journal after important sessions
- Product Owner has final decision authority
